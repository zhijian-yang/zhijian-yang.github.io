Yellow

We went to OHBM conference in Singapore city in 2019 with my colleagues. Right before the party began, we bought some beer and sat on a bridge to enjoy the night. There was a singer singing in front of us. I went to ask if I can use his guitar to sing a song. Voila...

Here is the original version from Coldplay that I like most! I think Yellow is my most sung song in front of friends or in public. All this brought me back to when I was singing in the subway in Beijing when I was in university.

 

 

 

Adieu - 后会无期

This was a performance for celebrating Chinese new year at Cité internationale universitaire de Paris, where I lived during my entire PhD. I was so lucky to be able to live at this fantastic place!

Back to the song itself, this Chinese song is the theme song of a Chinese movie (The continent) and adapted from the original song (The end of the world). The same melody but with different lyrics. My personal understanding for this song is: We meet, fall in love with, get apart. All those things may be unpredictible, but we should continue our journey and cherish the moments.
